// FAQ

Got Questions?

Nomians is a collection of 5,000 pixel art characters on the Base blockchain. Each Nomian is a one-of-a-kind digital collectible with its own unique combination of traits — crafted with artistic intention to stand out as a distinct piece of digital art.
Connect your Base-compatible wallet (MetaMask recommended, set to Base Mainnet) using the "Connect Wallet" button in the Mint section. Select the number of NFTs you'd like, then click "MINT NOW". Confirm the transaction in your wallet and your Nomian will be delivered directly to you.
Nomians lives on Base Mainnet (ERC-721 standard), deployed via OpenSea's SeaDrop protocol. Base is an Ethereum L2 built by Coinbase — offering fast, low-cost transactions with Ethereum-level security.
During the public mint phase, you can mint up to 100 NFTs per transaction. This ensures fair distribution while accommodating collectors who want multiple Nomians.
After minting, your Nomian NFT will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea (Base collections are fully supported). You can view, trade, or showcase it on any Base-compatible NFT platform.
