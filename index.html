What is PIXELHOOD?

10,000 Pixels.
One Hood.

PIXELHOOD is a collection of 10,000 algorithmically generated pixel art characters, each with their own unique combination of traits, colors, and personality. Living forever on the Base blockchain, every Pixelhood character is a one-of-a-kind digital collectible.

More than just art — PIXELHOOD is a community. Holders gain access to exclusive events, future drops, and the ever-growing Pixelhood ecosystem. Owning a Pixelhood means owning a piece of the hood.

// FAQ

Got Questions?

PIXELHOOD is a collection of 10,000 unique pixel art characters stored on the Base blockchain. Each character is algorithmically generated with a unique combination of traits, making every single one a rare, one-of-a-kind digital collectible.
Connect your Base-compatible wallet (MetaMask recommended) using the "Connect Wallet" button in the Mint section above. Select the number of NFTs you'd like to mint, then click "MINT NOW". Confirm the transaction in your wallet, and your Pixelhood character will be minted directly to your wallet.
PIXELHOOD lives on the Base blockchain (ERC-721 standard), deployed via OpenSea's SeaDrop protocol for a secure and reliable minting experience.
During the public mint phase, each wallet can mint up to 10 NFTs. This limit ensures fair distribution across the community.
After minting, your PIXELHOOD NFT will appear in your wallet and on OpenSea. You can also view it on any Base-compatible NFT marketplace or wallet that supports ERC-721 tokens.
